Why Natural Stone Matters in Feng Shui

In the practice of Feng Shui, materials carry weight. Stone represents the Earth Element, which is essential for grounding, stability, and patience. Unlike resin or mass-produced plastic statues, hand-carved stone holds the vibration of the earth.

When placing a stone Buddha in your home, you are literally anchoring the room's energy. This is particularly beneficial in high-stress environments like home offices or city apartments.
